# Checklists

Kickoff checklist

- Complete briefing template and confirm one-liner
- Agree on V1 scope and Not Yet list
- Identify primary stakeholders and approvals
- Confirm tools and communication cadence
- Establish initial milestones and acceptance criteria

Handover checklist

- Finalize documentation and architecture diagrams
- Deliver runbook and operational notes
- Confirm knowledge transfer sessions scheduled
- Ensure test coverage and quality gates met
- Obtain client sign-off on deliverables

QA checklist

- Define test scope and acceptance criteria per deliverable
- Schedule integration and system tests
- Verify performance and security requirements
- Record defects and assign owners
- Confirm fixes and regression testing before sign-off
